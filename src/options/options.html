Privacy & Data Notice

When using Ask ChatGPT, Ask Gemini, or Ask Grok, your clipped content is sent directly to your chosen AI provider. Please note:

  • No Storage: This extension stores nothing. No page content, no API keys on any server — everything stays on your device.
  • Data Transfer: Content leaves your device and is processed by the AI provider (OpenAI, Google, or xAI).
  • Personal Info: Avoid clipping pages with sensitive health, financial, or private credentials.
  • Your Responsibility: You are responsible for ensuring compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other data laws.

Help

How Synto works and keyboard shortcuts.

How it works

  1. Open the extension on any web page — Synto clips the page content to Markdown.
  2. Pick a template category (Understand, Decide, Act, or Compose), then choose a preset.
  3. The preview shows the filled-in prompt. Click Ask AI to send it to your configured provider.
  4. Ask follow-up questions in the chat panel below the response.

Template categories

Understand
Summarise and analyse — articles, tickets, pull requests.
Decide
Weigh options, trade-offs, and recommendations.
Act
Extract actions, risks, and blockers from discussions.
Compose
Draft replies, emails, and rewrites.

Tips

  • Selection mode — highlight any text on the page before opening Synto; only that selection is sent as {content}. Use this to focus on one section of a long page.
  • Lazy-loaded pages (Bitbucket, large diffs) — some pages only render content as you scroll. Scroll through the whole page first, then press Ctrl+A (or ⌘A) to select everything, then open Synto — it captures the selection automatically.
  • Refresh — the ↻ button re-clips the current page if the content changed after the panel first opened.

Keyboard shortcuts

⌥ + ⇧ + C
Open Synto
Opens the side panel from any tab
⌥ + ⇧ + ↩
Ask AI
Option + Shift + Return — when the panel is focused, sends to ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok (whichever is selected)

⌥ is the Option key (labelled alt on some keyboards). The Ask AI shortcut works only while the Synto panel has focus.
